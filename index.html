About me


Hi there! I’m a Computer Science and Engineering student who loves exploring the exciting intersection of creativity and technology. My journey in design started with Canva, where I discovered my passion for bringing ideas to life visually. Since then, I’ve been diving into Photoshop, learning new techniques, and experimenting with other design tools to keep improving and expanding my skill set.


Design, for me, is all about telling a story through visuals—creating something that catches the eye and resonates with people. I enjoy working in collaborative environments where ideas flow freely, and I can contribute my creativity while learning from others.


I’m always looking for opportunities to grow, try new things, and take on challenges that push me to be better. Whether it’s designing graphics, exploring new tools, or refining my craft, I’m excited to see where this journey takes me and how I can make a meaningful impact through design.
